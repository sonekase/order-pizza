Papi's Pizzera

The best pizza in Seattle, you can feel the love and pride in each of our pizzas. The ingredients we use are of the highest quality. We use fresh whole milk mozzarella cheese, San Marzano tomatoes imported from Italy, handmade sausage and fresh vegetables. Our toppings have intense flavor, so we recommend that you select only a few for your pizza so as to taste them each individually.

We keep our pies simple so your palate can enjoy the perfect marriage of ingredients and craft that is a Papi's Pizzeria Pizza. Mangia!
